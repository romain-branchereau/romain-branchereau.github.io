Romain Branchereau


Since August 2023, I am a postdoc at McGill with Henri Darmon. Before that, I was a postdoc at University of Manitoba with Siddarth Sankaran. From 2019 to 2022, I was a PhD student at École normale supérieure under the supervision of Nicolas Bergeron and Luis Garcia . I did my bachelor and master at ETH Zürich.
